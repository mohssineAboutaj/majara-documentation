Empty PSD Files?Back to Top

To save on upload capacity, I started conducting hide entire Groups when i save some new PSD Files.

How you can turn on Group and view all Layers?

It is super easy. You need to open Photoshop. Go to Windows > Layers (F7) to show the Layers Panel. In The Layers Panel, Click The Eye Icon. You will turn on Groups of Layers. Enjoy.
